Ingestion pipeline · sources → StromaDB

streaming

Many sources fold through two on-ramps into typed facts (provenance-stamped), resolve into a shared ontology (Types + predicates), and stream into StromaDB, kept live by incremental maintenance. Particles are data in flight. The LLM runs only at authoring time — the streaming hot path is token-zero.
